Our Story

Born in Mexico City,
raised in New Orleans.

Chilangos brings the soul of CDMX street food to the streets of Kenner, Louisiana. We're named after the proud people of Mexico City — the Chilangos — who carry a deep culinary tradition wherever they go.

From the bustling taquerías of La Condesa to the mariscos stalls of Tepito, every dish on our menu is a love letter to authentic Mexico City cooking. Now seasoned with New Orleans spirit — bold, unapologetic, and full of flavor.
